Computer Science is a broad field of knowledge that deals with the study of the theory, development, and application of
computer systems software and hardware. Including various disciplines such as algorithms, data structures, artificial
intelligence, theory of computation, programming, computer architecture, networking, databases, and many
others, computer science covers a wide range of topics related to the creation and use of computing systems.Computer
science professionals may be involved in software development, hardware systems design, artificial intelligence
research, data analysis, cybersecurity, web application
development, and many other aspects related to information systems technology.
